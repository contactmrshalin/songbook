How to Read Notation

The Seven Natural Notes (Shuddh Swar)

SargamWritten AsWesternSolfege
सा (Sa)SaCDO
रे (Re)ReDRE
ग (Ga)GaEMI
म (ma)maFFA
प (Pa)PaGSO
ध (Dha)DhaALA
नि (Ni)NiBTI

Shuddh Ma is written in lowercase as ma to distinguish it from Tivra Ma (Ma).

Komal Swar (Flat Notes)

NoteWritten AsWestern
Komal ReRe(k)Db
Komal GaGa(k)Eb
Komal DhaDha(k)Ab
Komal NiNi(k)Bb

Tivra Swar (Sharp Note)

NoteWritten AsWestern
Shuddh MamaF
Tivra MaMaF#

Octaves & Symbols

MIDDLE:  Sa Re Ga ma Pa Dha Ni  (C D E F G A B)
LOW:     pa dha ni              (lowercase)
HIGH:    Sa' Re' Ga'            (quote suffix)
KOMAL:   Re(k) Ga(k) Dha(k) Ni(k)
TIVRA:   Ma = F#

:  = hold/sustain    ~  = glide
|  = bar line        () = grace note
