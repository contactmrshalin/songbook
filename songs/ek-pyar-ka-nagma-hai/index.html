About

This timeless classic is a poignant reflection on life, love, and the bittersweet nature of existence. Its melancholic yet hopeful melody, beautifully rendered by two legends, has resonated across generations, making it an anthem for introspection and emotional depth. The song's simple yet profound philosophy is wrapped in a captivating musical arrangement that remains unforgettable.

  • 1 The song is famously picturized on Manoj Kumar and Nanda, conveying a deep sense of longing and philosophical acceptance.
  • 2 Lyricist Santosh Anand made a significant mark with this song, establishing his reputation for writing deeply meaningful and poetic verses.
  • 3 While often heard as a duet, the film features two distinct versions: one sung by Lata Mangeshkar and another by Mukesh, each capturing a different facet of the song's emotional landscape.
  • 4 It is considered one of Mukesh's most iconic and enduring songs, showcasing his unparalleled ability to convey profound emotion through his voice.
