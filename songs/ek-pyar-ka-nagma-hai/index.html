Song Information

Raag: Yaman Kalyan (based)

Thaat: Kalyan

About

This iconic song is a philosophical masterpiece, beautifully rendered by the legendary voices of Lata Mangeshkar and Mukesh. Its poignant lyrics by Santosh Anand, set to Laxmikant-Pyarelal's melodious composition, explore the transient yet beautiful nature of life and love. The song's serene melody, rooted in Raag Yaman Kalyan, evokes a sense of introspection and emotional depth, making it a timeless classic.

  • 1 The film 'Shor' features two versions of this song: a melancholic solo by Lata Mangeshkar and the more famous philosophical duet by Lata Mangeshkar and Mukesh.
  • 2 This song is considered one of Mukesh's last major hits before his untimely demise in 1976, cementing its place as a significant part of his legacy.
  • 3 Lyricist Santosh Anand received a Filmfare Award nomination for Best Lyricist for this song, which became one of his most celebrated works.
  • 4 Laxmikant-Pyarelal's composition masterfully blends the classical structure of Raag Yaman Kalyan with accessible film music sensibilities, creating a tune that is both sophisticated and universally appealing.
