About

This iconic ghazal is a masterpiece of Indian film music, beautifully rendered by the legendary Jagjit Singh and Chitra Singh. Its melancholic yet deeply romantic melody, set to Javed Akhtar's poignant poetry, evokes a profound sense of introspection and longing. The song's simple elegance and emotional depth have cemented its place as a timeless classic, resonating with generations of listeners.

  • 1 The film 'Saath Saath' was a low-budget production that gained cult status primarily due to its soulful music, especially this ghazal, which became synonymous with the film's identity.
  • 2 This ghazal marked one of the early collaborations between Jagjit Singh and lyricist Javed Akhtar, forging a partnership that would later produce many more memorable songs.
  • 3 The melody of 'Tum Ko Dekha Toh Ye Khayal' is often noted for its basis in Raag Shivranjani, a pentatonic scale known for its soothing and contemplative mood, perfectly complementing the song's emotional core.
  • 4 Jagjit Singh's unique style of ghazal rendition, characterized by his clear diction and emotive delivery, brought ghazals from niche classical circles to mainstream popularity through songs like this.
