Song Information

Film: Om Shanti Om (2007)

Singer: KK

Music: Vishal-Shekhar

Lyrics: Vishal Dadlani

About

Ankhon Mein Teri Sargam is a soulful and romantic ballad from "Om Shanti Om", beautifully rendered by the late KK. Its melancholic yet hopeful melody perfectly captures the protagonist's unspoken affection and longing, making it a timeless anthem for unrequited love. The song's gentle orchestration and KK's emotive vocals create an intimate atmosphere, drawing listeners into its poignant narrative.

  • 1 Vishal-Shekhar crafted a simple yet effective arrangement, relying heavily on acoustic guitars and a soaring string section to build its emotional arc, making it stand out amidst the film's more upbeat numbers.
  • 2 Vishal Dadlani's lyrics beautifully articulate the feeling of seeing a 'sargam' (musical notes) in someone's eyes, symbolizing the profound inspiration and music that love brings into one's life.
