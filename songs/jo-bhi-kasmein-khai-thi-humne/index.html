How to Read Notation

The Seven Natural Notes (Shuddh Swar)

SargamWritten AsWesternSolfege
सा (Sa)SaCDO
रे (Re)ReDRE
ग (Ga)GaEMI

ध (Dha)DhaALA

Shuddh Ma is written in lowercase as ma to distinguish it from Tivra Ma (Ma).

Octaves & Symbols

MIDDLE:  Sa Re Ga ma Pa Dha Ni  (C D E F G A B)
LOW:     pa dha ni              (lowercase)
HIGH:    Sa' Re' Ga'            (quote suffix)

TIVRA:   Ma = F#

:  = hold/sustain    ~  = glide
|  = bar line        () = grace note
