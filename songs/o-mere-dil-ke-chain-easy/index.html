Song Information

Film: Mere Jeevan Saathi (1972)

Singer: Kishore Kumar

Music: R.D. Burman

Lyrics: Majrooh Sultanpuri

About

An iconic romantic ballad, "O Mere Dil Ke Chain" perfectly captures the essence of 70s Bollywood romance. Kishore Kumar's inimitable voice brings to life R.D. Burman's timeless melody, creating a song that remains a beloved classic for its heartfelt emotion and enduring charm. Its simple yet profound lyrics resonate with anyone who has experienced the pangs of love.

  • 1 This song is considered one of Kishore Kumar's most iconic and defining performances, solidifying his status as the 'Voice of Romance'.
  • 2 Composed by the legendary R.D. Burman, the song showcases his genius in crafting catchy yet emotionally rich melodies that transcend generations.
  • 3 It was picturised on superstar Rajesh Khanna and actress Tanuja in the film 'Mere Jeevan Saathi', becoming one of the most memorable on-screen romantic numbers of its era.
  • 4 Despite the film itself having a moderate run at the box office, its soundtrack, particularly 'O Mere Dil Ke Chain', was a massive success and remains a classic in Indian cinema music.
